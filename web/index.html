About Us

Welcome to Club Malabari — where every bite is a journey into the true flavors of Malabar. We specialize in traditional Kerala cuisine, especially Malabar biryani made the authentic way. Our recipes are passed down through generations, using the freshest spices, marination, and cooking techniques. Whether you're craving chicken biryani, seafood, or traditional Kerala sides — we have it all.
